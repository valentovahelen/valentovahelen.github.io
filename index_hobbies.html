Hobbies

Travelling

One of my passions is traveling. I love the feeling when you get somewhere you’ve never been before and you can just see how the world and local live is completely different from our home.


I like spending time in cities and in nature equally. Both can provide you completely different experience. In nature you can be in peace and admire beautiful mountains, rivers and plants. But in the cities you can feel local culture on every corner.


So far I travelled mostly around Europe and this year I stared discovering east Asia.

Art

Art was my hobby since I was a child. I enjoy visiting galleries or just watching my favourite artists on social media. Besides that I try to create my own art. Mostly I draw and use ink. Sometimes I paint with acryl or watercolours.


But I still need a lot of practise to became better artist and I am considering to start learning digital art, which could be quiet handy with the web designing I mentioned in my skills.
